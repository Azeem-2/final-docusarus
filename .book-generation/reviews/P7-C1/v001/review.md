## Pass 3: Citation and Source Verification

### Coverage

- Underlying research file provides 11 documented sources (7 Tier 1, 4 Tier 2), including IFR reports, academic surveys, and vendor case collections.
- The draft correctly reflects high‑level trends from these sources (concentration in manufacturing, growth in logistics/service, sector patterns).

### Issues and Recommendations

1. **In‑text references**  
   - **Issue**: IFR and academic surveys are mentioned conceptually, but there are few explicit attributions (“according to IFR’s World Robotics report…”).  
   - **Recommendation**: Add 3–5 short in‑text references to anchor key claims (global distribution, growth of logistics, workforce findings).

2. **Policy/ethics references**  
   - **Issue**: Workforce and ethics sections are conceptually correct but could cite at least one major policy/ethics report (OECD, ILO, national academies).  
   - **Recommendation**: In the workforce/ethics section, mention one or two named reports to strengthen credibility.

3. **Vendor case balance**  
   - **Issue**: Vendor examples (e‑commerce warehouses, cobot case stories) are used appropriately but should not dominate.  
   - **Recommendation**: Ensure vendor‑specific details are framed as examples, with IFR/academic sources as the main evidence base.

Overall, citation quality is acceptable for a synthesis chapter; revisions are **minor** and do not block publication.
