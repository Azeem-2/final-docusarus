## 1. Introduction – Learning from Real-World Humanoids

- Why case studies matter: Learn from real-world implementations.  
- Real-world motivation: Understanding how concepts from earlier chapters are applied.  
- Key robots: Optimus, Figure 01, Atlas.

## 2. Analysis Framework

- Hardware specifications: Size, weight, actuators, sensors.  
- Locomotion capabilities: Walking, balance, terrain adaptation.  
- Manipulation capabilities: Hands, grasping, dexterity.  
- HRI capabilities: Communication, interaction.  
- Safety systems: Safety features and mechanisms.  
- AI/Control: Control architecture, learning methods.

## 3. Tesla Optimus

- Overview: General purpose bipedal humanoid.  
- Hardware: Tesla-designed actuators and sensors.  
- Locomotion: Walking capabilities, Gen 2 improvements.  
- Manipulation: Hand capabilities, grasping.  
- AI/Control: Autonomous operation, AI-powered control.  
- Applications: Tesla facilities, general purpose tasks.

## 4. Figure 01

- Overview: OpenAI-powered conversational humanoid.  
- Hardware: Human-like dimensions, 20 kg lifting capacity.  
- Locomotion: Bipedal walking capabilities.  
- Manipulation: Hand capabilities, object manipulation.  
- HRI: Speech-to-speech reasoning, conversational AI.  
- Applications: General purpose humanoid tasks.

## 5. Boston Dynamics Atlas

- Overview: Dynamic humanoid research platform.  
- Hardware: Advanced actuators, sensors.  
- Locomotion: Dynamic motions, RL-based control.  
- Manipulation: Whole-body manipulation, hands-on tasks.  
- AI/Control: Reinforcement learning, autonomous behaviors.  
- Applications: Research, advanced manipulation tasks.

## 6. Comparative Analysis: Design Philosophy

- Optimus: Mass production, general purpose, Tesla ecosystem.  
- Figure 01: AI-first, conversational, OpenAI integration.  
- Atlas: Research platform, dynamic capabilities, advanced manipulation.

## 7. Comparative Analysis: Capabilities

- Safety comparison: Safety features and mechanisms.

## 8. Comparative Analysis: Applications

- Optimus: Industrial, manufacturing, general purpose.
