## Section 2: Defining Digital Twins

**Word Count**: 600–800  
**Purpose**: Provide a clear, student-friendly definition.  

### Content Elements
- Definition: a **living digital representation** of a physical asset, process, or system, synchronized via data streams.  
- Key properties: real‑time data, bi‑directional connection, lifecycle coverage (design → operation → decommissioning).  
- Contrast with offline simulations and static CAD models.
