## Research Question

What hardware building blocks underpin sensors and perception for mobile and manipulator robots, and how should we organize an educational chapter (P2-C2) that introduces these components in a way that connects cleanly to later perception, simulation, and project chapters?

## Key Areas to Cover (Scaffold)

1. Core sensing modalities: encoders, IMUs, force/torque sensors, proximity sensors, cameras, depth sensors, LiDAR.  
2. Signal paths: from analog phenomena to digital signals (sensing, conditioning, A/D conversion, interfaces).  
3. Mounting and field of view: how sensor placement, calibration, and field of view affect perception quality.  
4. Perception pipelines: how raw sensor data flows into state estimation, mapping, and higher-level perception.  
5. Practical constraints: noise, bandwidth, latency, dynamic range, environmental robustness, cost.  
6. Safety and reliability: redundancy, health monitoring, and failure modes for critical sensors.
