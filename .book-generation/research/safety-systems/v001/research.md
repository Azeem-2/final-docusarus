4. How do we implement safety monitoring and fault detection?

## Key Concepts Identified

### Core Concepts
- **Safety systems**: Mechanisms to ensure safe operation
- **Collision avoidance**: Preventing collisions with humans and objects
- **Force limits**: Limiting forces to safe levels
- **Emergency stops**: Immediate shutdown mechanisms
- **Safety monitoring**: Continuous monitoring of safety conditions
- **Fault detection**: Detecting and responding to faults

### Techniques
- **Collision detection**: Detecting potential collisions
- **Force sensing**: Monitoring contact forces
- **Safety zones**: Defining safe operating zones
- **Redundancy**: Backup systems for critical functions
- **Fail-safe design**: Systems that fail safely

### Workflows
- Safety monitoring → Detection → Response → Recovery
