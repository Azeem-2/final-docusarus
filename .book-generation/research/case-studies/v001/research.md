## Key Concepts Identified

### Tesla Optimus
- **General purpose bipedal humanoid**: Designed for unsafe, repetitive, or boring tasks
- **Tesla-designed actuators and sensors**: Custom hardware
- **Faster walking and hands**: Improved Gen 2 capabilities
- **Autonomous operation**: AI-powered control
- **Production plans**: Limited production in 2025, 1,000+ units in Tesla facilities

### Figure 01
- **General purpose humanoid**: First-of-its-kind AI robotics company
- **OpenAI technology integration**: Speech-to-speech reasoning
- **Human-like dimensions**: Approximately same size as human
- **Lifting capacity**: Can lift 20 kg
- **Conversational AI**: Seamless conversations with humans

### Boston Dynamics Atlas
- **Dynamic motions**: Exceeds human capabilities
- **Reinforcement learning**: RL-based policies
- **Whole-body manipulation**: Autonomous manipulation and locomotion
- **Hands-on tasks**: Autonomous manipulation tasks
- **Research platform**: Advanced research and development
